Final Project

The program I decided to create is a game for entertainment called Catch The Stars.
How to Play: Press the space bar to catch the drifting stars
and once you catch 50% of the total stars the game is over.

Incorporated in the game is a pause button as well as a timer
and a score tracker. The scene is set during the night and the point
of the came is to catch the stars as they drift away. I had gotten the idea from a mix of hoppy beaver
as well as the frogger game. Included some aspects like the pause and timer and score limit to reach.
